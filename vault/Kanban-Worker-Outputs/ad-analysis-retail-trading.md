## 1. Top 5 Pain Points Targeted

| Rank | Pain Point | Why It Resonates |
|------|------------|------------------|
| 1 | **FOMO / Missing the Move** | "Everyone else is making money on [AI / memes / 0DTE] except me" — the #1 emotional driver in retail trading. |
| 2 | **Information Asymmetry** | "Institutions / whales / insiders know something I don't" — retail traders feel structurally disadvantaged. |
| 3 | **Analysis Paralysis / Noise Overload** | "Too many tickers, too many opinions, no clear signal" — decision fatigue is real. |
| 4 | **Blowing Up Accounts / Risk Management** | "I keep taking small wins and one massive loss wipes me out" — the silent killer of retail accounts. |
| 5 | **Lack of Edge / "Gambling" Feeling** | "Am I trading or just guessing?" — desire for a repeatable, data-backed process. |

## 4. Offer Structures (What's Actually Being Sold)

| Tier | Typical Package | Price Anchor | Key Value Props |
|------|-----------------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Free / Lead Magnet** | Discord access, weekly watchlist, free scanner tier, email course | $0 | Builds list, demonstrates signal quality, community hook |
| **Entry ($29–$99/mo)** | Real-time alerts, unusual options flow, crowd sentiment dashboard, basic education | $49/mo | "Coffee per day," cancel anytime, immediate ROI framing |
| **Pro ($149–$299/mo)** | Full signal suite, proprietary indicators, 1:1 onboarding, trade reviews, API access | $199/mo | "Institutional tools for retail," dedicated support |
| **Lifetime / Annual** | All-access forever, future features locked in | $1,497–$2,997 | "Never pay monthly again," scarcity-driven |
| **Prop / Funded Track** | Evaluation account, scaling plan, profit split | $197–$497 eval fee | "Trade our money, keep 80%," clear path to full-time |

**Common Guarantees / Risk Reversals:**
- "7-day free trial, no card required"
- "30-day money-back if you don't see value"
- "Cancel anytime, keep the education"
- "Profitability guarantee: if you follow signals and lose, we refund + $100" (rare, high-trust)
