## Production Notes

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Talent** | Founder/lead analyst (authentic, not polished "guru" vibe). Real traders from Discord for UGC clips (with permission). |
| **Music** | Low-fi beat, 85–95 BPM, subtle tension → resolve on CTA. No corporate stock music. |
| **Captions** | Burned-in, high-contrast, kinetic (word-by-word pop on key phrases). 9:16 safe zones. |
| **Compliance** | Disclaimer overlay last 3s: `Not financial advice. Past performance ≠ future results. Options involve risk.`
| **Aspect Ratios** | Deliver 9:16 (Reels/TikTok/Shorts), 1:1 (Feed), 4:5 (IG Feed), 16:9 (YouTube In-Stream). |
| **Localization** | Captions ready for ES/PT/FR/DE overlays (top geo: US, UK, CA, AU, DE, BR, IN). |
